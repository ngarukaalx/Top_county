About Us

Welcome to TopCounty, your number one source for all things related to county information. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of data, with an emphasis on accuracy, timeliness, and comprehensiveness.

Founded in 2024 by Hiram Kabuagi, TopCounty has come a long way from its beginnings in Kenya. When Hiram first started out, his passion for county transparency drove them to start their own business.

We hope you enjoy our information as much as we enjoy offering it to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
